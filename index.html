Greasemonkey UserScript for FritzBox

This userscript works with my FritzBox 7490 running Fritz!OS 6.90.

Tested in Greasemonkey, may also work in Tampermonkey. Modern browsers only, as it uses MutationObserver

It adds a small button to unknown numbers in your call list, so you can do a revers lookup for that number by simply clicking on it.

You can find the script here.
